Physical Controller Design

Our group decided to use pennies that served as conductors to make each individual person a different keyboard command. Then we had one person in the center who wore aluminum foil act as the player. The player would touch the other members and move through keyboard commands in a game.

Design Concept + Inspiration

My main inspiration for this game was from an old popular youtube video about a type of bird called a kiwi. To summarize without spoiling too much of the video, the kiwi just wants to fly. So, I made a simple game where the kiwi must avoid trees and gather feathers to earn points.
